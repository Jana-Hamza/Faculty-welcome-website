Welcome To The Faculty Of Physical Therapy

The Faculty of Physical Therapy at Suez Canal University is committed to preparing skilled professionals in the field of physical therapy. Our programs combine academic excellence with hands-on clinical training, enabling students to meet the demands of healthcare systems locally and internationally. We welcome all new students and wish them a successful academic journey.

About Us

Faculty Of Physical Therapy

The Faculty of Physical Therapy at Suez Canal University in Ismailia was established following the approval of the Supreme Council of Universities under decision No. 2146 of 2019. The Faculty welcomed its first students in the academic year 2023/2024. It grants a Bachelor’s degree in Physical Therapy after completing five years of study, in addition to a one-year internship, under the credit hour system. The program consists of a total of 194 credit hours, plus 8 university requirement credit hours.

Students Activities

The Faculty encourages student activities that promote personal growth, teamwork, and leadership skills. Students can participate in cultural, scientific, sports, and volunteer events. These activities aim to create a balanced university experience and strengthen the connection between students and the community.

Scientific Conferences

The Faculty supports student participation in scientific conferences, whether by attending or hosting events. These conferences provide opportunities to exchange knowledge, present research, and connect with professionals in the field of physical therapy and related sciences.

Laboratories

The Faculty provides well-equipped laboratories to support practical training and skill development. These include the Digital 3D Anatomy Laboratory, the Measurement and Evaluation Laboratory, the Therapeutic Exercises Laboratory, and the Electrotherapy Laboratory. Students use these facilities to gain hands-on experience and apply theoretical knowledge in a practical setting.

Outpatient Clinics

The Faculty of Physical Therapy at Suez Canal University operates the Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Center to serve patients and train students. The center offers specialized clinics for orthopedics, sports injury rehabilitation, pediatric rehabilitation, and neurological disorders. It is equipped with advanced medical and rehabilitation devices, providing high-quality services at affordable rates while acting as a practical training platform for students.
